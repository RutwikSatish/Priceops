Most CPQ tools validate
after the quote is signed.
In most B2B sales operations, pricing mistakes reach the customer because discount validation happens manually, after the fact, by someone checking a spreadsheet against a policy PDF. PriceOps enforces policy in real time — every override is checked against the partner tier's maximum before the quote can be submitted. The UAT Validator tests pricing rules before they go live, the same way an SAP or Salesforce CPQ implementation would be validated before a rollout.

Consultant Verdict
REAL PROBLEM CPQ workflow fragmentation (Salesforce + SAP + spreadsheets) is a documented pain point at mid-market B2B companies. Policy mistakes reaching customers is a real, costly problem.

STANDOUT FEATURE The UAT Validator is the most technically differentiated element across all four projects. Running 8 structured test cases across 5 business units mirrors actual SAP CPQ implementation testing. This is a Business Analyst / Pricing Ops credential — not just "built a dashboard."

AUDIT TRAIL The audit log with flagged overrides is the right governance structure. Shows awareness of SOX-adjacent compliance requirements that pricing operations teams deal with daily.

NOTE Gross margin in CPQ uses total-level discount as proxy rather than line-level cost rollup. Acceptable for a demo tool — be ready to explain this in a technical interview.

SYNTHETIC DATA Entirely fictional — Nexus Semiconductor, 15 invented SKUs (DC-GPU-001, etc.), 10 simulated partner accounts. Discount tier numbers are designed, not pulled from real channel programs. Frame as: "Demo models a realistic semiconductor channel program — architecture mirrors Salesforce/SAP CPQ tier structures."

Resume framing: Full CPQ lifecycle with SAP/Salesforce-parity UAT validator and SOX-ready audit trail. Signals enterprise pricing operations and system implementation awareness.
